El Salvador Must Go To Destinations

Located in Central America is the small country of El Salvador. It is covered with various volcanoes, lakes, and destinations that one must visit.

Lago Coatepeque

located in the western part of the country is a lake called Coatepeque, which is known for turning from a blue to turqouis color depending on the season. This is a great place to relax and spend the day, since you are able to visit one of the many restuarnts that are along the lake's shore and swim at one of their piers. You are also able to rent out some jetskis or take a boat tour around the lake making this place have a nice option of having fun or relaxing.

Santa Ana Volcano

For those seeking more of an adventure, climbing up a Santa Ana's volcano might just be what your looking for. Climbing up this volcano will be a day long activity since it usually takes anywhere from 6-8 hours round trip to complete. Once on top of the volcano you will be delighted to know that there is usally a man selling icecream to accomplish your treck. The crater of the volcano is filler with a sulfer lake turning it into a bright turqouis color. On top of the volcano you will be able to see the neighboring volcano Izalco and the lake Coatepeque.

Ruta de las Flores

Ruta de las Flores, also known as the Flower route is a series of small colonial towns that one can visit in a day or a weekend if you wish to stay at an inn in one of the towns. The roue is made up of 5 towns with each being different from the last. Here you will be able to find authethic street food, visit the towns catherdral, and explore the town's history. Some of the towns even offer waterfalls to treck to making this trip a place to experience the culture, food, and people.

Suchitoto

Located in the center of the country is the colonial town of suchitoto. The area in which this town resides in offers various experiences from exploring the irregular waterfall structure of "Cascada los Tercios" to the breath taking view of the lake behind it at Casa 1800. The town offers a varity of places to go and explore which is why one might want to consider spending the night at one of the local inns in order to fully expierence what this town has to offer.

De Cameron Salinitas

For those who want to experience the beach with some night life should consider visiting the resort De Cameron located in Salinitas. This all in one resort will privde you with free meals, unlimited drinks, a beachside room, obstacle courses, kayaking, and a very own discoteque club to visit at night. This place will truly leave you feeling relaxed with loads of oppertunites to have fun.
